# MedConnect – Intelligent Doctor Recommendation & Appointment Management System

MedConnect is a web-based healthcare platform that streamlines the process of connecting patients with the right medical specialists and managing appointments efficiently. Unlike traditional appointment booking systems, MedConnect incorporates a symptom-based assessment quiz that helps patients identify the most suitable specialist before scheduling a consultation.

The system analyzes patient responses to a guided questionnaire and recommends appropriate specialists such as Gynecologists, Cardiologists, Dermatologists, Orthopedic Specialists, Neurologists, and General Physicians. This reduces confusion, improves healthcare accessibility, and helps patients make informed decisions regarding their medical needs.

## Features

* Secure patient, doctor, and administrator authentication
* Symptom-based healthcare assessment quiz
* Intelligent specialist recommendation system
* Online appointment booking and management
* Doctor profile and schedule management
* Patient profile management
* Appointment history tracking
* Admin dashboard for managing doctors, patients, and appointments
* Responsive and user-friendly interface
* Role-based access control and session management

## How It Works

1. Patients register and log in to the platform.
2. A health assessment quiz collects information about symptoms and concerns.
3. The system evaluates the responses and determines the most appropriate medical specialist.
4. Relevant doctors are displayed based on the recommendation.
5. Patients can book appointments directly with the recommended doctor.
6. Doctors manage appointment requests through their dashboard.
7. Administrators oversee the entire system and maintain platform data.
